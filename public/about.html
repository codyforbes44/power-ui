Claude Power UI is a privacy-first AI workspace that puts you back in the driver's seat. We believe your conversations, your context, and your workflow should belong to you — not to a subscription service that profits from your data.

Bring your own API key and own your costs directly. Fork the MIT-licensed repo and extend every corner of the interface. We don't dumb things down — we expose the full depth of Claude's capabilities and hand the controls to the people who know how to use them.

Our Philosophy

Three principles we never compromise on.

🔒

Privacy by Design

Your prompts and conversations stay on your machine or your chosen API endpoint. We collect zero telemetry, track zero analytics, and store zero cloud-side history. Local-first isn't a feature — it's the architecture.

Open Source

The Story

Why we
built this.

It started with frustration. Every mainstream AI product launched with the same playbook: pay a monthly subscription, hand over your conversations as training data, accept whatever interface the design team approved that quarter, and be grateful when a new feature trickles out behind a paywall. We thought developers deserved better.

The trigger was watching skilled engineers accept crippled context windows, opaque rate limits, and zero visibility into what was actually being sent to the model — all because the alternative was self-hosting something that required a PhD in DevOps to stand up. We wanted a middle path: a polished, production-quality interface that was still completely transparent and infinitely extensible. Bring your own Anthropic key, connect it in thirty seconds, and get full access to every token.

Claude Power UI was written in the open from day one. The philosophy is simple — if you can read the code, you can trust the code. If you can fork the code, you can own your workflow forever. No vendor lock-in, no price hike risk, no opaque data policies. Just a fast, beautiful, deeply capable workspace that respects the intelligence of the people using it.

Manifesto

What we believe.

Four convictions that guide every decision we make, every line we write, every feature we ship.

  1. 01

    Your data is yours. Full stop.

    No product should ever profit from the intellectual work you produce in an AI session. Conversations, code, ideas — they belong to you and no one else. We enforce this by design, not by policy.

  2. 02

    Subscriptions shouldn't lock you out of your own AI.

    Paying Anthropic directly for API access is cheaper, more transparent, and more powerful than any intermediary subscription. BYOK means you choose your spend, your limits, and your model versions.

  3. 03

    Transparency is a prerequisite for trust.

    Closed-source AI interfaces are a black box between you and the model. Open source means every prompt template, every API call, every piece of middleware is auditable. Trust through verification, not through marketing.

  4. 04

    Developers deserve interfaces that respect their intelligence.

    The best tools don't hide power — they make power accessible. We expose the full surface area of what Claude can do and trust that developers know how to wield it responsibly.
