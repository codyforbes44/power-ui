FAQ

Pricing questions

You pay your AI provider directly at their standard rates — no markup from us. For example, Claude 3.5 Sonnet costs ~$3/MTok input, $15/MTok output at Anthropic. A typical power user spends $5–20/month on API calls, often less than a subscription. Cost tracking is built into the app so you always know your spend.
Yes. Cancel at any time from your account dashboard. You keep access until the end of your billing period. For annual plans, we offer a prorated refund within the first 30 days. No questions asked.
Your data is yours. You can export everything before downgrading from the Admin dashboard. After downgrading, the cloud-hosted instance shuts down, but you can always run the app locally — all features remain available on Free when self-hosted.
Yes — Pro and Team both come with a 14-day free trial, no credit card required. You'll be prompted to add a payment method at the end of the trial if you want to continue.
Yes. We offer 50% off Pro and Team for verified students, nonprofits, and open-source maintainers. Contact us via GitHub with proof of eligibility.
